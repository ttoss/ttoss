## Job description

<InitialJobDescription />

Your main goal as [Junior Full Stack Developer](/docs/engineering/career/roles#junior-full-stack-developer) is to learn our [workflow](/docs/engineering/workflow) and our [technologies](/docs/engineering/technologies) and to be able to work on our projects and became a [Mid-Level Full Stack Developer](/docs/engineering/career/roles#mid-level-full-stack-developer).

## What we expect from you

<JuniorFullStackDeveloperRequisites />

### As a team member:

1. To be transparent, expose your ideas and opinions, and be open to feedback, helping our team to grow.
1. To be curious and always available want to learn new things.

### Technically:

1. Understand and split big tasks into smaller ones and work on them.
1. Handle easy to complex tasks using [our stack](/docs/engineering/technologies).
